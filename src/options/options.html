Here you'll find various options for you to choose from. There is an option for changing theme and there is also an option for changing font. The options will truly allow you to experience DOM Distiller the way you want to.

Color

There are three different colors to choose from. We've got Light which is our most popular color. The reason for that might have something to do with the fact that it is the default theme.

Other than Light we've also got the Dark theme. It might suit those who get eye strain from spending much of their time starting at screens

Finally we've got the Sepia theme. It has a retro feel to it and might suit time travelers from the past.
